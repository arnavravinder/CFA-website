Our Mission

CareForAll (CFA) is a non-profit organisation that was birthed with the mission of providing 1000+ of the underprivileged with healthcare and awareness.

Along our journey, we have realised that we have the potential to help several more and spread our roots into new communities. Thus, we have reformed our mission statement to impacting 1000+ of the underprivileged annually.

What Makes Us Unique

At CareForAll, we hold medical camps and awareness sessions where we educate children on simple, effective hygiene techniques they can easily incorporate into their lives; we also inform and register adults for government schemes they can directly benefit from simultaneously.

We firmly believe that it is necessary to hold both medical and awareness sessions, as while the health camp restore the health conditions of individuals, our awareness sessions serve to maintain these stable conditions and prevents families from returning to the same deteriorating conditions.

Additionally, from the medicines and follow-ups for each individual to the food and drinks we provide at camps and sessions, we fund everything ourselves, leaving the NGOs and schools without any expense of their own.

What We've Done

By mobilising and collaborating with doctors from top-tier hospitals—including Sakra World Hospital and Ramaiah Medical College—engaging student volunteers, and collaborating with well-known NGOs, CareForAll has been able to impact lives through medical camps and awareness sessions.
