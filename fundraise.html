Why Your Support Matters

Every rupee you contribute directly impacts lives. Your donation funds medical camps, medicines, awareness sessions, and follow-up care for families who otherwise have no access to basic healthcare. We operate with complete transparency—100% of your donation goes towards our mission, with no administrative overhead charged to donors.

Our Commitment to Transparency

Every camp expense is documented and shared with donors

Regular impact reports with photos and testimonials

Direct communication with our team about fund utilization

Option to sponsor specific camps or initiatives

How Your Donation Helps

₹500

Provides complete health checkup, medicines, and follow-up consultation for one child including deworming tablets, basic medicines, and nutritional supplements.

₹2,500

Sponsors a comprehensive medical camp for 10 children with doctor consultations, vital checks, medicine distribution, and health education materials.

₹10,000

Funds a complete awareness session and medical camp for 50 families including hygiene kits, educational materials, government scheme registrations, and follow-up support.

₹25,000

Sponsors an entire community health initiative reaching 100+ families with medical camps, awareness sessions, library setup, and three months of follow-up care.

Where Your Money Goes

Medical Supplies (40%)

Medicines, first-aid supplies, diagnostic equipment, deworming tablets, nutritional supplements, and health monitoring devices.

Awareness Materials (25%)

Educational posters, hygiene kits, demonstration materials, books for rural libraries, and printed guides for families.

Camp Operations (20%)

Venue arrangements, volunteer transportation, food and refreshments for participants, equipment rental, and camp setup costs.

Follow-up Care (15%)

Tracking patient progress, additional consultations, specialist referrals, continued medication supply, and long-term health monitoring.
