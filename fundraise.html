Why Your Support Matters

Every rupee you contribute directly impacts lives. Your donation funds medical camps, medicines, awareness sessions, and follow-up care for families who otherwise have no access to basic healthcare. We operate with complete transparency.

Our Commitment to Transparency

Every camp expense is documented and shared.

Regular impact reports with photos and testimonials.

100% of donations go to the mission.

Where Your Money Goes

Medical Supplies (40%)

Medicines, first-aid supplies, diagnostic equipment, and nutritional supplements.

Awareness Materials (25%)

Educational posters, hygiene kits, and printed guides for families.

Camp Operations (20%)

Logistics, transportation, and camp setup costs.

Follow-up Care (15%)

Tracking patient progress and additional consultations.
